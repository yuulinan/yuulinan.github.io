Welcome!

I'm a doctoral student in Economics at Colorado State University.

My research interests are in health economics, demographics, regional economics, and the economics of aging. My goal is to address questions relevant to policy by applying a diverse set of machine-learning techniques and econometric methods including dynamic panel data estimators, natural experiments, and microsimulations.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Economics and Business Administration from Coe College in 2022.
