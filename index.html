Welcome!


I'm an Assistant Professor of Data Analytics at Dickinson College.
My research uses data science to answer questions related to behavior in competitive environments. Additionally, I work on topics related to consumer behavior and natural disasters.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of South Carolina in May, 2021.
